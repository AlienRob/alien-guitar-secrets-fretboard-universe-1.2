# FRETBOARD UNIVERSE

## THE CALL OF THE GUARDIANS

### 60-SECOND CINEMATIC INTRO

### 4 x 15-SECOND SEGMENTS

STYLE

AAA game cinematic.
Photorealistic.
Epic cosmic fantasy.
Premium mobile game quality.
Mystical and inspiring.
Adventure and hope.

---

## SEGMENT 1 (0:00–0:15)

### THE GALAXY OF MUSIC

VISUAL PROMPT

Black screen.

A single over-driven guitar musical note echoes in the darkness.

An indigo portal slowly opens.

The camera flies through the portal into a breathtaking galaxy made entirely of music.

Gigantic planets spin slowly in space.

Legendary guitars fly past the camera like celestial ships.

Constellations form notes, chords and fretboard patterns.

Wormholes connect distant worlds.

The camera sweeps through the galaxy at high speed.

CAMERA

Begin on black.

Push through the portal.

Fast cinematic fly-through of planets and guitars.

VOICEOVER

"There was once a time when music was limitless. A universe where creativity, knowledge and expression lived among the stars."

SFX

Deep cosmic drone.
Portal opening.
Orchestral rise.
Distant guitar harmonics.

---

## SEGMENT 2 (0:10–0:25)

### THE MUSICAL CRISIS

VISUAL PROMPT

The galaxy begins to dim.

The colourful planets lose their glow.

Musical symbols crumble into dust.

A dull grey waveform appears.

The same four glowing chords repeat endlessly around the galaxy.

The planets stop spinning.

The guitars begin falling from the sky.

CAMERA

Slow movement through the fading galaxy.

VOICEOVER

"But a great crisis has begun. Across Earth, music is being forgotten. The age of the endless four-chord song has arrived. Notes are being ignored. Scales are fading. Intervals and harmony are being lost. Soon... musical theory itself may become a thing of the past."

SFX

Music slows.
Low impacts.
Distorted echoes.
Fading harmonics.

---

## SEGMENT 3 (0:25–0:40)

### THE KEEPER OF THE GALAXY

VISUAL PROMPT

Suddenly, an indigo portal erupts with light.

Zashtar emerges holding Wearing the Galactic Masterpiece Guitar

The owl appears upon his right shoulder.

The wolf materialises over his lefy shoulder.

His indigo third eye glows brightly.

The dead galaxy slowly begins relighting behind him.

CAMERA

Slow push-in toward Zashtar keeping beyong the owl & wolf in shot.

VOICEOVER

"I am Zashtar... Keeper of the Galaxy and Guardian of the Ancient Frequencies. You have been chosen because of your love of music... and your love of the guitar. Only those who truly care for the art can restore what is being lost."

SFX

Portal energy.
Mystical choir.
Subtle heartbeat.
Rising orchestral swell.

---

## SEGMENT 4 (0:40–0:55)

### THE MISSION

VISUAL PROMPT

Ten glowing planets ignite around Zashtar.

The silhouettes of the ten Guardians appear.

Ingvar.
Shreddy.
Hansy.
Hemi.
Mo.
Levi.
Nena.
Sandy.
Arygmor.
Vairon.

Zashtar raises the AGS Galactic Masterpiece guitar.

The guitar glows with indigo and emerald energy.

An interactive fretboard appears as a hologram around it.

Notes, intervals, scales and chords illuminate the galaxy.

CAMERA

Orbit around Zashtar.

Slow push into the glowing guitar.

End with Zashtar extending the guitar toward the viewer.

VOICEOVER

"Your mission is clear. Train with the Guardians. Master your notes. Learn your intervals. Understand your scales and chords. Only then can you return to Earth and begin righting the wrongs of this musical crisis."

(Pause)

"Take the AGS Galactic Masterpiece. The fate of music now rests in your hands."

NO TEXT ON SCREEN

FADE TO BLACK.

SFX

Epic orchestral climax.
Electric guitar harmonics.
Magical energy surge.
Final cinematic impact.
