ORIGIN STORY

Born beneath the twin suns of Valkyria Nova, Nena Craus displayed extraordinary speed and determination from an early age.

While others relied on talent, Nena devoted herself to relentless training, mastering every challenge placed before her.

After conquering the legendary Velocity Peaks and defeating the Mechanical Titans of Nova Sector Seven, she unlocked the Celestial Velocity Core, granting her unmatched control, focus and endurance.

Today she serves as one of the most feared and respected Guardians in the Fretboard Universe.
