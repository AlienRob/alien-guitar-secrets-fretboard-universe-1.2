I would choose neither completely.

I'd use a hybrid system, because it gives you the excitement of a boss battle without frustrating users.

Recommended System
Level Up Unlocks The Boss Planet

Player gains XP through:

Daily challenges
Notes
Intervals
Scales
Chords

Once enough XP is earned:

🌍 Boss Planet appears.

Player can now enter the Boss Battle whenever they choose.

Boss Battle Required For Completion

The next solar system remains visible but locked.

Message:

"Defeat the Guardian of Sirius to open the Wormhole."

This creates anticipation.

Boss Battle Rewards

Boss defeated:

🏆 Trophy Guitar

🏅 AGS Belt Promotion

🌀 Wormhole Opens

🌌 Next Solar System Unlocked

🎁 Huge XP Bonus

This becomes the player's major achievement moment.

Why I Wouldn't Use Optional Bosses

If it's optional, many users will skip it.

Then you lose:

Achievement
Tension
Celebration
Memorable milestones

The boss becomes irrelevant.

Why I Wouldn't Hard Gate Pure XP

This can be frustrating.

Imagine:

Player earns enough XP.

Player sees next system.

Player loses boss battle.

Player loses boss battle.

Player loses boss battle.

Player quits.

Not ideal.

One More Thing

The boss should never be impossible.

If the player fails:

Show:

⭐ Highest Score

⭐ Personal Best

⭐ Progress Towards Victory

Example:

Required Score: 80%

Your Best: 74%

Now they immediately want another attempt.

That's the psychology that keeps people playing.

So my recommendation to the agent would be:

"Boss Planet unlocks through XP progression, but defeating the Boss is required to open the Wormhole and enter the next Solar System."
