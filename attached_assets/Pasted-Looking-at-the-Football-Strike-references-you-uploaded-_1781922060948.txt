Looking at the Football Strike references you uploaded, they are not actually hinged like a real duffel bag.

They use a bit of visual trickery:

Closed State
Bag is almost a rectangular cube rather than a cylinder.
Perspective is a 3/4 top-down view (camera above and slightly in front).
One long zipper runs along the top centreline.
The zipper pull sits on one side (usually left).

Opening Animation

When tapped:

Zipper pull slides from left → right
Entire top panel lifts upward from the centre seam.
Front and rear edges stay rigid.
Interior appears suddenly underneath.
Lid opens around 70–90°.

Think of it more like a treasure chest lid than a soft duffel bag.

Why yours keeps looking wrong

The AI keeps generating:

soft cylindrical gym bags
two-way zippers
centre-opening lids
floppy fabric

Football Strike bags are actually:

✅ Squarish boxes
✅ Semi-rigid walls
✅ Single zipper pull
✅ Top lid opens as one piece
✅ Overhead 3/4 perspective
✅ Interior is a rectangular cavity
