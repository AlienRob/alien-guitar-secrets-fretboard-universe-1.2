The biggest mistake guitar apps make is turning everything into a quiz.

Nobody wakes up thinking:

"I can't wait to answer note flashcards today."

People come back because they're playing a game, getting rewards, and improving without realising it.

🎯 Note Game – "Galactic Note Hunt"
Core Idea

The fretboard is dark.

A mission appears:

"The planet is powered by G notes."

All G notes begin hidden around the neck.

Player has:

⏱️ 30 seconds

Tap every G.

Make it fun

✅ Correct note explodes into purple energy.

✅ Combo meter:

3 in a row = x2

6 in a row = x3

12 in a row = Cosmic Mode

✅ Wrong note:

-3 seconds

Combo resets.

Variations
Asteroid Mode

Notes fall onto the fretboard.

Tap only the requested notes.

Example:

Destroy:

A notes

Avoid:

Everything else.

Like Guitar Hero meets Space Invaders.

Fog of War

Only part of the fretboard is visible.

As notes are found:

More of the neck reveals.

Creates exploration.

Survival Mode

No timer.

One mistake = game over.

How many notes can you find?

Perfect for leaderboards.

🪐 Scale Game – "Scale Constellations"

This could become your signature game.

The fretboard is a night sky.

Player receives:

"Create A Minor Pentatonic."

Every correct note becomes a star.

As notes are discovered:

Constellations form.

When complete:

The stars connect.

The whole pattern glows.

A cosmic guitar chord rings out.

Extremely satisfying.

Different Scale Modes
Pattern Builder

Build scale notes anywhere.

Path Finder

Start:

5th fret Low E.

Navigate through the scale to:

17th fret High E.

Cannot touch wrong notes.

Like a maze.

Speed Climb

Scale notes appear.

Tap in order:

1
2
b3
4
5
b7

Beat the clock.

Scale Runner

Avatar runs across frets.

Only stepping on scale notes keeps moving.

Wrong note:

Character falls into space.

Kids will love this.

Boss Version

Guardian:

Vairon – Architect of Sound

Challenge:

"Build the Dorian Scale."

Every correct note weakens him.

Wrong notes restore his health.

When completed:

Massive cosmic explosion.
